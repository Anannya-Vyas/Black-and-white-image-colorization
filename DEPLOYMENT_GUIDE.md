#🚀 FINAL STEP: How to Put Your AI Colorizer Online

You've done the hard part! Now, follow these simple steps to launch your website on the internet for free.

---

### Step 1: Push the final code to GitHub
I have already added the "magic" files (`render.yaml`, `Dockerfile`, etc.) to your folder. I will now push them to your GitHub repository.

### Step 2: Create a Free Render Account
1. Go to **[dashboard.render.com/register](https://dashboard.render.com/register)**.
2. Click **"GitHub"** to sign up using your GitHub account.

### Step 3: Launch Your Website!
1. Once logged in, click the **"New +"** button (usually at the top right).
2. Look for **"Blueprint"** in the list and click it.
3. You will see a list of your GitHub repositories. Click on **`Black-and-white-image-colorization`**.
4. Render will look at the `render.yaml` file I created and automatically set everything up.
5. Click **"Apply"** or **"Approve"**.

---

### ⏱️ What happens next?
- **Building**: Render will start building your app. You'll see a black screen with some text flowing—that's all normal!
- **Model Download**: Our AI model is 123MB. Render will download it automatically during the first launch. This might take 1-2 minutes.
- **Done!**: Once it says "Service is live," you will get a link (like `https://ai-image-colorizer.onrender.com`).

**That's it! Your AI Colorizer is now public!** 🎨✨
